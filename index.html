My personal interests:

My first interest is that I like to work on electronics. I spend a lot of my time creating prototypes for IoT devices that I deploy around my house. For example, I created an alarm system for my house with network cameras and notifications that I can look at on my phone. I also like to create FPV drones and fly them around. It's a lot of fun to work on electronics.

My second major interest is reading. I love reading all sorts of books, partricularly sci-fi fantasy books. I like the Dune series books, as well as the Cosmere books by Brandon Sanderson. I also really enjoy non-fiction such as philosophy and psychology, or really any book where I can learn something new.

Finally, I am very interested in Cybersecurity. Since I like to program, I like the idea of keeping those programs secure, which is what brought me to be interested in cybersecurity for networks, cryptography, and web security. I also love to explore cryptography and learn about different cryptography schemes, particularly the math behind them. I've been studying lately on post-quantum cryptography which is absolutely fascinating.
